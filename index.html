About Me

Hello World! A genius, billionaire, playboy philanthropist, are words I'd wish described me, but I am simply a web developer with an overzealous imagination that has seen too many movies. I develop websites both front and back end, dabble with AI, and prototype with 3D printers, but I still don’t know how taxes work. I believe some of the best programming is done on paper, really. Putting it into the computer is secondary. Google is my weapon of choice, but I also know some JavaScript, HTML, CSS, C#, PHP, FML, and IDK.

When I’m not coding I’m sleeping, and when I’m not doing either I’m filming. Yes, filming. Premier Pro and After Effects are my guilty pleasure, as well as photoshop. I use film and photography in my creative process and link them into my websites whenever possible. I’m a big believer in showing a story rather than telling one, film and photography allow me to do that. Gaming is also a hubby of mine, and I mean creating games not playing games. I find creating a game is sometimes more fun than the actual game. Now if you’re looking for a web developer with a lot of time in his hands hit me up, or if you just want to hang out, I’m down too.
